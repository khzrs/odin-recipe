Omelette Recipe

Cheese Omelette

Perfectly cooked omelette, the cheese element allows it to remain soft and a little thick, even when folded it remains a little fluffy

  • 2 or 3 Eggs
  • Chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Cilantro
  1. Crack the eggs open into a bowl
  2. Remove any shells that may have fallen in
  3. Whist the eggs until smoothish liquid
  4. Chop cilantro and add into eggs
  5. Add a pinch of salt
  6. Sprinkle a generous amount of chilli powder
  7. Whisk further to mix everything together
  8. Turn a cooker corner on to a low heat
  9. Use a little butter to line the pan
  10. Once the butter melts a little, pour the egg mixture into the pan
  11. Sway the pan around to make sure the mixture is evenly spread around
  12. You can increase the heat ever so slightly, and once it starts mildly solidifying, sprinkle in some cheese
  13. Flip the omelette in the pan, allow both sides to be slightly done
  14. Fold the omellete over and once cooked to your liking, place in plate and serve!
